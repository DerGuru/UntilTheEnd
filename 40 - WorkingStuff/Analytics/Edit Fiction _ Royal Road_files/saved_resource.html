She is older than civilization. More powerful than gods. And she longs only for death.

But something won‘t give her peace.

When a twelve-year-old farm boy follows her and will not turn back, she discovers — for the first time in thousands of years — a reason to live.

But everything she loves dies. Always.

- - -

What to expect:

A dark xianxia / progression based fantasy with an overpowered female lead and a strong long-arc payoff.

Character-driven tension over pure spectacle: growth, pressure, and hard choices.

A calm, mature tone with dry humor, introspection, and emotional restraint.

Meaningful progression: each step changes relationships, stakes, and worldview.

A world that feels mythic but grounded, with atmospheric settings and cinematic mood.

Action that focuses on impact and consequences, not empty fireworks.

Slow-burn depth with high-end payoff.
