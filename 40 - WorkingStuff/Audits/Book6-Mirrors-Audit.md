## 12. Issues ‚Äî Severity Ranked

### ‚ùå CRITICAL

**Issue #1: Wei's Age at First Meeting**
**Location:** Ch12 (6-2-04-12), line 29
**Quote:** *"A child ‚Äî eight, maybe nine, the age where humans are large enough to be independent and small enough to be vulnerable"*
**Problem:** Per B2 audit, Wei is 12 at B1 start and turns 13 during B2. If B1 is where Yun and Wei first meet, then "eight, maybe nine" is a continuity error of 3‚Äì4 years. If this is a pre-B1 memory (Yun observing him years earlier from afar), this needs clearer framing ‚Äî the current text reads as "the moment I first saw him," which readers will associate with the B1 meeting.
**Recommendation:** Either (a) change to "twelve, maybe thirteen" to match B1, or (b) add a single clause clarifying this was years before B1 events: *"before I approached, before the story"* or similar.

**Issue #2: Escalating POV Breaks (First ‚Üí Third Person)**
**Location:** Ch37, 38, 41, 43, 47, 48, 50
**Description:** At least 12 instances of "she/her" used for Yun in narration that is otherwise first-person. The drift escalates: isolated in Part 6, sustained passages in Ch43, and the final chapter (Ch50) ends in pure third person: *"She walked. He followed."*
**Impact:** If unintentional, this is a significant craft error in a book that has been otherwise impeccable in POV. If intentional (bridging to B7's third-person shift), it needs a single anchor moment where the narrator acknowledges the distance ‚Äî the shift feels unconscious rather than purposeful.
**Recommendation:** Decision required:
- **If intentional (bridge to B7):** Keep Ch50's ending. Add a brief narratorial self-awareness moment in Ch43 or 44: Yun noticing she's thinking of herself in third person. Fix the isolated slips in Ch38, 47, 48 (these read as errors, not intentional drift).
- **If unintentional:** Restore all instances to first person. Consider keeping only Ch50's final three lines as a deliberate closing gesture.

### ‚ö†Ô∏è HIGH-SEVERITY

**Issue #3: Cultivation System Not Explicitly Recontextualized**
**Description:** The outline states the twist should reframe all B1‚ÄìB5 meta-moments, and Ch25 does this for Gedanken-Echos, Monolog-Spaltung, anomalies, and self-protection. But the formal cultivation hierarchy (Qi Condensation ‚Üí Immortal) ‚Äî a major worldbuilding element across B1‚ÄìB3 ‚Äî is never addressed.

**Description:** The outline specifies ~60‚Äì75 chapters. The delivered text has 50. Part-by-part: P1 = 8 (outline: 7‚Äì10 ‚úÖ), P2 = 7 (outline: 8‚Äì12 ‚ö†Ô∏è), P3 = 7 (outline: 8‚Äì12 ‚ö†Ô∏è), P4 = 6 (outline: 10‚Äì14 ‚ùå), P5 = 6 (outline: 8‚Äì12 ‚ö†Ô∏è), P6 = 9 (outline: 8‚Äì12 ‚úÖ), P7 = 7 (outline: 7‚Äì10 ‚úÖ).

**Recommendation:** Consider whether 2‚Äì3 additional chapters in Part 4 would strengthen the twist's landing or dilute its compression. The current execution favors density over space ‚Äî this is a valid choice but worth conscious confirmation.

**Issue #5: Third-Person "She" Ambiguity in Voice Integration (Parts 6‚Äì7)**
**Description:** In Ch37, 38, and 41, "she" is used both for Yun's deeper self ("She. Me. The distinction was dissolving") AND as accidental third-person narration. ## 13. Observations ‚Äî Narrative Choices (Not Issues)
