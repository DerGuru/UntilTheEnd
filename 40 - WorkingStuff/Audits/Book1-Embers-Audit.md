# Book 1 "Embers" — Continuity & Consistency Audit

## Executive Summary

Book 1 is **remarkably consistent** across its 66 chapters and 7 Parts. Hard numbers (village population, house count, trench length, Wei's age, cultivation stages) are used correctly throughout without contradiction. The timeline reconstructs cleanly to approximately three months, confirmed by an explicit reference in 1-7-08-62. POV discipline holds — no breaks from first-person retrospective narration detected. Motifs (hands, outer robe, third-step pattern) are tracked and evolved correctly.

**Five issues were found,** ranging from minor typos to a moderate continuity gap. None are structural or plot-breaking.

### Issue 4 — ⚠️ CONTINUITY: Wei's siblings vanish after Part 3
Older brother appears in 1-2-04 and 1-3-04. Sister appears in 1-3-04. Neither mentioned in Parts 4–7 during drought, evacuation, or tribulation. No explanation given.

### Issue 5 — 🔍 NARRATIVE CHOICE: Father's death off-screen
Terminal illness established Parts 1–2. Death mentioned retroactively in 1-6-05: "Wei's father had died a month ago." No scene, no grief beat at the time. May be intentional (Yun's voice) but worth conscious confirmation.
