# Answer
Your are absolutely correct, that it is AI written. At least most of the text is. I edited quite a lot. 
In my other two books, I was more upfront with it in a preface, but they never gained momentum, so I did not bother this time. It was basically just for fun, to see if I could "write" a series, which amounted to coming up with one big idea and enough smaller ones, to support the seven books I had planed for it from the get go. It also meant, that I would have to it through ... Until the End.

I never imagined it getting more than a few views, like the others. And then - over easter - it started trending and the story gained momentum on RR. Which I never thought possible.

I must object to your "default style"-comment though. I put a lot of effort into style and voice. Making sure, that each individual has their own voice and each book in the series its own tone. So yeah, even though there is quite a lot AI involved. There is also a lot of me in there. 

I understand, if you don't like the book itself and give it a 3-Star-Rating, but please don't downvote, just because there is AI involvement. It already happened twice and I think, it does not deserve that.
