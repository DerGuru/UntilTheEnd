Xu Ran stood at the clearing's edge. His face was the pale of revised understanding — a shade darker than usual, because this time the revision was not about the impossible. This time the revision was about something else.

He opened his notebook. He closed it. He opened it again.

This was the loudest thing Xu Ran had ever done.
