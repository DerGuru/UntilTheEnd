# AUDIT: Book 3, Part 7 — "Resolution" (Chapters 54–62)

> **Auditor:** Senior Editor
> **Date:** 2026-02-13
> **Scope:** 9 chapters (3-7-01 through 3-7-09), aftermath of Wei's death
> **Eiserne Regel:** NO flashbacks, NO memories, NO explanations. ONLY perception + action.
> **Target voice:** EMPTY, stripped, numb. Not analytical. Not philosophical.

---

## TL;DR — PART 7 VERDICT

Part 7 has the **right architecture** — every outline beat lands, the emotional trajectory is correct, "Was Wurzeln hat, stirbt langsamer" closes the book powerfully. But the **execution drowns grief in analysis**. Yun should be NUMB; instead she's a professor delivering a lecture on her own devastation. The "specific"-tic reaches pandemic levels (57 instances across 9 chapters). The E:R ratio is completely inverted (avg **25:75** vs. target 67:33). Post-showing cascades average **8.3 per chapter** — every physical detail gets an analytical autopsy. The Eiserne Regel is violated 3 times (historical memories in Ch59, "In Book 1" in Ch61, naming past characters in Ch56). The meta-references to "the next book" and "Book 4" in Ch58/Ch60 are heavy-handed structural commentary, not unconscious leaks.

**Core problem:** The prose EXPLAINS grief instead of BEING grief. Strip 60% of the reflexion. Let the brown grass and cold bowls speak for themselves.
