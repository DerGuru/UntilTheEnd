# AUDIT REPORT: Book 3 – Part 2 (Until The End: Silence)

**Auditor:** Senior Editor  
**Date:** 2026-02-13  
**Scope:** Chapters 3-2-01 through 3-2-09 (9 files; Chapter 10–18 internally)  
**Outline reference:** 3-2-Szenen.md (specifies ~10 chapters, 9 delivered)

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Part 2 carries forward **every systemic disease from Book 2** and in some dimensions worsens them. The prose has calcified into a single recursive structure — the "whose-chain" — that now constitutes the dominant rhetorical device in every paragraph of every chapter. The Event:Reflexion ratio is catastrophically inverted across all 9 chapters. Dialog deserts span the final two chapters entirely. Three consecutive chapters end on the same word ("gamble"). Two chapters contain explicit meta-narrative references ("two books") in a Part where the outline mandates **zero meta-moments**.

The narrative beats themselves are properly placed and emotionally resonant. The Shen Liang arc, the "Not just—" moment, Wei's return — these land. The problem is not what happens but how it's told: every beat is shown, then immediately buried under 2–5 paragraphs of analytical autopsy that explains to the reader exactly what they should have felt, killing the emotion it generated.

**Verdict: MAJOR REWRITE REQUIRED on prose texture, E:R ratio, and tic removal. Beat structure can be preserved.**
