## Part-Level Pattern Summary

### The "specific" Pandemic

### The Template Sentence
The core disease: **"the specific [NOUN] that [CLAUSE] produced/occurred/indicated/required"**. This template appears 80+ times. It is the prose equivalent of a PowerPoint bullet. It names a phenomenon, names the category, names the cause, and names the result â€” all in one sentence. This eliminates mystery, reader engagement, and narrative momentum simultaneously.
